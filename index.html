Botify

A Discord bot designed to give a shared music listening experience to users in the same voice channel. The Python program automatically searches YouTube for the best result the user inputted, and extracts the audio of that video to play for everyone in the call to listen to.

Touchless Halloweeen
Candy Dispenser

A no-contact way to give children Halloween candy and help mitigate the spread of COVID-19. The user waves their hand to the left of the device, and it will dispense a piece of candy for them.

Winner of the “Best Hardware Hack” category in the 2021 CUNY Hackathon, Box of Help is a voice-activated device designed to contact the emergency contacts of people who are in a situation where they can’t call for help themselves. This project was mainly targeted to help the elderly.
